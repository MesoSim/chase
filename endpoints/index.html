To enter the LSR endpoint into GR, add the below link in the placefile manager:
https://chase.iawx.info/endpoints/placefile_lsr.py

To enter the endpoint into GR, add the below link in the placefile manager:
https://chase.iawx.info/endpoints/placefile_team.py?team_id=your_team_id_here
(this link and your team id are available in the chase web application).
